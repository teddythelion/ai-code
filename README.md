
Ai-codelabs is a Next.js and Tailwind CSS web application that showcases the use of AI in various aspects of our lives. It's dedicated to helping people better their lives through the integration of AI, by embedding AI into different parts of their lives such as smart devices, IOT, cars, workflows, and more.
Its functionality is limited but will be fully animated whenin production this is for te purposes of showcasing front end skills.
